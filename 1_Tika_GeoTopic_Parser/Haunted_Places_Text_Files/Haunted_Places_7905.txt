Location: Honolulu

Description:
Waianae,Maili Coralsands - Across Maili 76 Gas Station - It has been told that the beach across the 76 gas station is haunted with animal spirits that were buried for many years since 1980's. You can here howling of dogs and meowing of cats...Even whispers from the ocean of people who died on the pier or in the waters. This beach is well known for a lot of ghostly and sounds activity. Waianae Coast area from Nanakuli to Makaha is also Well known for Hawaiian ancestors hauntings who had settled way back in the 1800 hundreds including the Heiau's and Makua cave in Makaha.
