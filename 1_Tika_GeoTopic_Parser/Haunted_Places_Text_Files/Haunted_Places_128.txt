Location: Michigan State University

Description:
Hubbard Hall - on the 12 floor of the South complex it is reported, the lights on the floor go out & hearing someone running down the hall. Upon investigation into the hall is deserted but footsteps and laughing are heard. Also reported an apparition of a man walking into the elevator and it goes down to the 9th floor when it gets there the door opens and you feel a cold breeze go past you. Everyone on the floor has experienced doors slamming, appliances going on and off, and windows opening and closing.
