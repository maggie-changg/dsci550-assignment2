Location: Brick Tavern

Description:
once a jailhouse for criminal miners, people claim to have felt a presence in the basement where the cells used to be. Noises can often be heard before closing time and a few have seen an apparition of a lost miner.- July 2005 Update: The jail cells where built as a movie set for the movie The Runner Stumbles with Dick Van Dyke is 1978 or 1979.
