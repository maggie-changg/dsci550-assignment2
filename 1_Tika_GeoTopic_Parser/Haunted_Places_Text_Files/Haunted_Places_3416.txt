Location: The University of Maine at Farmington

Description:
Mallet Hall Dorm - Haunted by a little girl. her name might be mary. she is harmless but she roams the halls. sometimes doors open and close when locked. No one knows why the little girl is there. she has been known to move people's things on their dressers. she has been known to move furnature into piles in the summer on the lower floors when people are staying in the upper ones. is said to be haunted by a dorm mother who hung herself. To this day, people have not been able to stay in the room that once was hers.
