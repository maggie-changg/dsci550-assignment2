Leatherwood - On a particular rd, Barrow's mill rd, in Henry co. there was a house called the "pink house".  This house was a rental property some time or another where many families grew up and moved on.  The house however has been demolished and there was an incident of a child being hit and run in a blind curve at this site.  the child appears in the middle of the curve with no warning and has been seen by numerous people
