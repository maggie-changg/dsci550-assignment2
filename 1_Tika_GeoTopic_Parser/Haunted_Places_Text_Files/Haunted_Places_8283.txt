Location: Old Hanging Ground

Description:
(off of Padgett Road, near Starr's Mill)  Old hanging ground that belonged to Old Man Padgett. During a hanging, a Negro broke loose and hung Padgett from the nearby railroad bridge. Padgett still walks the tracks looking for his killer. The track has been abandoned for 15-16 years and you can still hear train horns and see figures on the dirt road, tracks, and in the woods and hear voices in the woods.
