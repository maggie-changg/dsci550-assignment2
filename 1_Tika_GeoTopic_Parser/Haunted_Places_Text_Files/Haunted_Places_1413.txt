Location: Taft High School

Description:
In 1969,a young couple in their senior year of high school was brutally tortured and killed to death in the football field bleachers.Ghost sightings have been seen during football games.Two girls by the names of Elizabeth and Evelyn had once felt their legs being pulled while sitting in the bleachers. Once they looked down into the bleachers,they saw two faded shadows simply disappear at a glance.Numerous incidents have been reported all of which cannot be explained.
