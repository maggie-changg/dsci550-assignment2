Location: Courtyard by Marriot

Description:
Room 201 - This room is haunted by a man who died there, and whose body was not found for several days, according to the staff. Also, there is a ghost by the name of Ken who wanders the parking lot and courtyard, in and around the gazebo. There is also what appears to be a cat walking around the hotel. It's very evident in rooms 103 and 107.
