Location: Plymouth

Description:
Little Egypt - Many claims have been made of apparitions appearing at night at the cemetery. There have also been claims of driving around the cemetery and hand prints appearing on your windshield. One story is if you throw a nickel on a baby's tombstone, that you will hear it cry. Little Egypt is located on 5A Road and Hawthorn in Marshall County. 5A road goes around the cemetery in a square style. - June 2008 Update - Warning: The local residents do not appreciate investigators. And will chase you with their shotguns.
