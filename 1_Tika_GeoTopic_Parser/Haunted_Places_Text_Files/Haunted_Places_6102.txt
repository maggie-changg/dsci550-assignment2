Location: Iron Hill

Description:
In these woods behind the old white farm there have been sightings of a little girl dressed in white, also various girls have been heard. Around Halloween you can hear faintly a chanting that is something like this, "ring around the rosy..." and "wanna come burn with me". Around 1920 there was a train car full of orphans that burned down on the railroad tracks that run through there. a burned rag doll has been found. They say it is there graveyard yet no family ties have been found.
