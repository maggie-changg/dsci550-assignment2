Location: BH Playhouse – Costume Room

Description:
is said to be haunted by a girl whos father was a miner there be fore it became the play house.  And that the girl haunts the costume room because that’s the room she stayed in.  Some times the costume people will leave the room one night with every thing put away and the next morning on the table will be a costume laid out perfectly.  They say that its the girl who laid it there.
