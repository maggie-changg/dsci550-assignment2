Location: the Benton Homestead

Description:
Several entities have been seen, including-Jemima Barrows, betrothed to one of the Benton boys who died from smallpox, wandering the halls in her wedding dress, weeping (or just her weeping). -A Hessian soldier on the front steps -A man in Colonial-era clothes in the dining room (seen by psychics Ed and Lorraine Warren) -A malevolent presence on the second floor felt by one member of the Tolland Historical Society.
