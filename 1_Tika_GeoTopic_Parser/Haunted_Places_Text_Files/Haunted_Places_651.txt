Location: Twin Tunnels

Description:
Creek Rd off of Boot Rd in D-Town. Two tunnels connected by a sliver of open space with a wire connecting them. Stories say that a man hung himself from the wire. At the entrance (going towards boot rd.) spray painting of a suitcase with an arm sticking out pointing down. Writing by painting says "help me". it was pointing at a suitcase on the ground filled with a females dismembered body parts. She was said to have been killed by a motorcycle gang from the area in the early nineties.
