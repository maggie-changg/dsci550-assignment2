Location: Shafter Lake

Description:
There is a place known as Shafter Lake in between Seminole and Andrews. It is known to have been a Indian Burial grounds. It is said that if you go there at night and park just off the road by the large rocks and flash your lights three times you will see an apparition of a woman carrying a lantern dressed in a white flowing gown coming towards you. You can also hear the sounds of drums at the site.
