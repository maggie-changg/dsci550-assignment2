Description:
The Gym/ 2nd Auditorium - The gym which seconds as a auditorium has been reported to be haunted.  A death of a well-known basketball player was hit very hard by the school's faculty, staff and students.  Sightings of the passed athlete have been reported in the gym as well as on the outside courts, practicing for the next big game.  The gym was also the site of the death of an athlete (wrestling) that got his neck broken during a match.  He has been seen roaming the halls as well as running around the gym practicing.
