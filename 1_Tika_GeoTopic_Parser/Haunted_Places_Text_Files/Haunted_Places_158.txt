Location: School House Mall

Description:
Story has it, that in the late 1800's when the mall was still a school, a teacher was killed there. A murder was running loose and this unlucky teacher was right in harms way. She has been seen wandering the hallways trying to protect her students from the killer. - March 2005 Correction: St. Lorenz Lutheran grade school--was not built on that site until 1928.
