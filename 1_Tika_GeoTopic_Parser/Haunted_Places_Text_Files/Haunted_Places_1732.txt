Location: Taunton State Hospital

Description:
The Goss Building -  is definitely haunted. Staff have seen a man in white walking the halls of the third floor. Also, there is a corner room at the front of the building (third floor) that gives everyone a "creeped-out" feeling. - Never Trespass on any of these locations!!! Always get permission to enter abandon or private property. If you want to visit any of these locations please read this first.
