Location: railroad tracks

Description:
There have been many sightings of an old women searching for her family. Her family was pushed on the railroad tracks just as an on coming train swept by and there bodies were never found. The old lady hung her self in a house not to far from he tracks. In a road called Fowler Hollow a mile or so back, the body was taken to a morgue and the blood stains were covered up by new boards and the blood still can be smelled on certain nights. - October 2004 Correction: The house near the railroad tracks is on a road called Hannah Lane. It's a big abandoned green house. The house is in pretty bad condition. It's an original log cabin and where the front door is, the front porch is caved in. No trespassing! The police will be called.
