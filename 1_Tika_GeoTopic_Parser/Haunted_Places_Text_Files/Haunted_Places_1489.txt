Location: Bridge Road

Description:
This is a very haunted location. If you are coming off of rte 68 the mark is on the left side of bridge Road. A red X on a tree marks where a woman died in a car accident in 1995 do to an icy road. a white figure appeared gliding down the road. She is said to follow people back down the road until past the second cemetery on Rte 68 headed into Baldinville.
