Location: Stage Stop Ranch

Description:
If you go there at 12:00 to 2:00 you will see A ghost of a man hanging from a large oak tree that is found to the left of the main plaza. He was hung in the tree in the early 1800's for killing a sheriff's deputy. It is said his corps was left to hang until the buzzards picked him clean. You will also hear the wheels of the wagons that once past through.
