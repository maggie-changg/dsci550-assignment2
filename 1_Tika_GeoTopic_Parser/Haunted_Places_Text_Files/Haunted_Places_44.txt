Location: Point Iroquois Lighthouse

Description:
Point Iroquois Lighthouse, outside of Brimley is said to be haunted, there is a story about a ghost girl who was eaten by a bear, the bear was later killed and parts of the girl where discovered inside. The site is also the location of an Indian battle, which took place in the 1600's, where the Ojibwe and Iroquois had a battle, and the Iroquois's where slaughtered. The lighthouse has had cold spots and figures have been seen.
