Location: Sutersville /Shaner

Description:
The Church - Located in Sewickley Township, PA off Mars Hill Road. This is a location of a very old church that was burned down. There are many rumors that the church was used in the occult and that people and animals where killed there. The hillside which is now over grown is a cemetery and the ground is real soft there so watch your step if you enter this area. The church is not really accessible by vehicle you have to walk up the hill. When you get to the top of the hill you will see the steps the foundation and the alter of the church that is all that remains. Paranormal activity is very high in this location and there are many other haunts in this area.
