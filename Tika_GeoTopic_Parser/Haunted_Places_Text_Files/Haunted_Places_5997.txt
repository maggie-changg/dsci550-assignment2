Location: Wallkill Correctional Facility

Description:
It's a medium security state prison built in 1932. Both Corrections Officers and Inmates have reported seeing an inmate from years back appear and disappear. He is dressed in an old-style tan uniform. One Officer reported finding a button on the gallery's desk - from an old-style tan uniform. At the same time, an inmate saw a man go past the officer's desk -dressed in an old style tan uniform.
