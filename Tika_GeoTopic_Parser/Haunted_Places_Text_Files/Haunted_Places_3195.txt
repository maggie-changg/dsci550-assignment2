Location: crybaby bridge- Formerly listed in Checotah

Description:
the sight of a single lane ironwork bridge(now replaced by a new modern concrete and steel),many locals have told the story of hearing a baby's cry from beneath the bridge late at night and even some sightings of what appears to be a faintly glowing image of a woman hovering just above the rocky floor of the creek.- March 2004 addition: another story is In the late 40's A back woods man raped his daughter several times and instead of keeping the babies she threw them over the bridge into the North Boggy Creek.Legend Has it that if you go to this bridge and stand at the edge of it you can hear the babies crying.
