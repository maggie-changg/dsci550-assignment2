Location: Coal Spring

Description:
Hardburly is a old mining camp in Kentucky. It has legends of hauntings. One is Coal Spring, a stretch of road that lines Jake Branch Stream. Its here they say you can hear a child scream, with no one there. The story is a boy drown in flood waters there. He still screams. In 1970s a guy was chased by a un seen thing. Faster he ran it ran, just behind him. He stopped to catch his breath it was heard gasping for air also. After he got up a bank he did not hear it any more.
