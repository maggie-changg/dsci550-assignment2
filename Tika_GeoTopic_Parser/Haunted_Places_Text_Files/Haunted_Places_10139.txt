Location: Jackpot Bingo Hall

Description:
One witness that used to work at the bingo hall she is always in the store, and she always hears a little baby crying but then she looks in every lane to see if anybody is there but there wasn't. It was just her in the bingo hall while everybody are in their offices and outside smoking a cigarette. The bingo is built on a cemetery and you can see a lot of things moving around. Especially chairs, papers, and tables. She says she quit because of the phenomena.
