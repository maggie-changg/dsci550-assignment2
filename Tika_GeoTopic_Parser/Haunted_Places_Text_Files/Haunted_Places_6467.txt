Location: Haunted Gauntlet at Battersby Asylum

Description:
The land of evil spirits invites you to visit one of Washington States LARGEST Haunted Attractions (30,000 sq. feet). Loaded with special effects and animatronics, once you enter the depths of this industrial sized gauntlet, it'll spit you out altered. An Enumclaw Parks Dep. haunted attraction benefiting the E.H.S class of 2005 and others.
