Location: Rotherwood Mansion

Description:
Sometime back, a women lived in this house, and was looking for a husband. When she found one, he drowned on a fishing trip at Holston River on the wedding day. This happened several times to this girl, and soon she murdered herself. Now on foggy, full moon nights, people have reported 2 things. 1, they said they saw a women in a wedding dress in her room window, crying. 2, they saw a women in a wedding dress rushing down Holston River.
