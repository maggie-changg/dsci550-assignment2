Location: crybaby bridge

Description:
It has been said that a young woman threw her baby off the bridge and then jumped to her own death as a train was on the tracks below in mid November. it is also said that if you go across the bridge at midnight your car stalls on the bridge and you can hear a train whistle blow a baby cry and a woman scream after everything happens your car will start and you can go on your way. - June 2008 Update: torn down in early 2004. It has since been filled with dirt and turned into a road instead.
