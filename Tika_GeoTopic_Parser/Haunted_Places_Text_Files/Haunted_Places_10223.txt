Location: St. Anthony Hall Fraternity House

Description:
In the mid 1900's a member of St. Anthony Hall was killed in a car accident. Since his death members of the Fraternity report many strange instances: In his former room (the Sigma room) TV's have been reported turning on and off, unexplained noises coming from the walls and circling the room. On occasion, around 1 a.m., he has been seen in the Fraternity library. Strange footsteps and noises have been reported from employees keeping the house during the unoccupied summer months.
