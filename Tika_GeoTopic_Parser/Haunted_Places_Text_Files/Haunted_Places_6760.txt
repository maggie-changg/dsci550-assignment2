Location: US HWY 20

Description:
If you are driving on US Hwy 20 around 12:00am till 5:00am you might as you near the Brushy Prarie Cemetery You may get a glimpse of the Lady in White. She wonders down the highway towards the cemetery in a beautiful white wedding dress. Many people have tried to pick her up, but when they pull up to her she just disappears. She is most active around holidays.
