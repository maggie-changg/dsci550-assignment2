Location: Polk City

Description:
Fantasy of Flight Aircraft Museum - Owner collects WW1 and WW2 military aircraft. Most of the aircraft were found after being shot down. Voices, footsteps, cold spots can be experienced. A Pilot has been reported as being seen hanging in a cockpit of a crashed plane that is exhibited exactly as found. On 9/11/01 employee reported seeing 2 men dressed in ww2 uniforms walking on tarmac and entering one of the hangars.
