Location: Tyler House

Description:
What is now a teenage recovery house is the site of the Leonard House were a family lived during the gold rush in the mid 1800s, but moved to Aptos after a while. There was also a Jesuit Mission in the area, both have since been torn down and the Tyler House stands in it's place. The beach near by is also haunted by a boy who drowned there in the 1960s. The Tyler House residents have experienced noises and objects moving as well as reports of windows opening and closing on their own, a very haunted house.
