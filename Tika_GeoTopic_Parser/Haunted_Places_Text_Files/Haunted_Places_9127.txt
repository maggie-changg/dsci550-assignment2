Location: Green Olive Interiors

Description:
This interior design store was once one of the oldest homes in Keller. It is alleged to be haunted by its original owners. A paranormal investigative group witnessed a whiskey bottle falling over by itself and dowsing rods being pulled from an investigator's hands. Reports include neighbors seeing an aparition appearing in a window of the building, a ball of orange light floating through the air, voices, strange noises, and odd smells.
