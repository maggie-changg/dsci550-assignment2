Location: Kennesaw House

Description:
Used as a hospital during the Civil War, now a museum, people have seen ghosts of soldiers here. An entire hospital "scene" completes with soldiers in their hospital beds and 2 people witnessed doctors working with them at the same time in the basement area or lower floor. Employees have also heard several unexplained noises. While on a field trip of the Marietta Museum located in the Kennesaw House, a girl reported seeing a woman dressed in a light blue antebellum style dress with pink flowers around the neckline and bodice. This woman smiled at her and then vanished. She claims it is a woman in a picture at the museum. The picture is of the original owners of the Kennesaw House.
