Location: Old Bryce Hospital

Description:
Writings on the walls, strange noises, and cold spots are some of the phenomena that occur in this old mental institution. - September Update: Upon entering this old institution, there are strange feelings of presences watching you. Throughout the entire building(3 floors and a basement)there are writings on the walls, and lots of graffiti. It was recently burned on the first and second floors, but not severe. Reports of telephones ringing, furniture being moved, footsteps heard in the hallways, and cold and hot spots throughout. You just get an odd an eerie feeling in the building. There is no need for any high tech equipment here. You can feel everything you need to know. Patrolled on Halloween and sometimes during the week. You could be caught for trespassing.
