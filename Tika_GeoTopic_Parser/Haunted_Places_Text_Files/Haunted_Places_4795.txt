Location: Arlie Boggs Elementary

Description:
their used to be downstairs bathrooms in the school back in the late 1900's, but after many reports on a baby crying down their and due to the need for new bathrooms more where built up stairs and the bottom one sealed off. Now at night you can hear a baby crying and the sound of an older woman, thought to be the mother, saying, "LET ME OUT", and beating on the floorboards.
