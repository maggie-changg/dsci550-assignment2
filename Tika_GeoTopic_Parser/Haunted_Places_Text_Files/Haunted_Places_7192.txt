Location: Main Street Graveyard

Description:
It is located in the outskirts of Pendleton, Indiana. Anderson is at least 20 miles away from it. And Main Street Graveyard is located a half mile down the road from that. It is where a little girl is suspected to be the devil. On top of her grave there has been a plant growing over many of years. this plant has now begun to shape like the Devil's Pitchfork.
