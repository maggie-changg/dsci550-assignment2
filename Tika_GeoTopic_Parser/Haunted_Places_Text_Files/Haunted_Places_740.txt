Location: Grove City College

Description:
The Lilly Room - Locked off room in bottom of girls dorms and cafeteria. Rumored that a young girl hung herself in the closet of the room. The room was closed off 15 yrs later opened up again and the smell of lilies the same smell of the girls perfume lingered. The room was sealed off again. Sightings of the girl at night on lower floors of dorms. Pounding can be felt sometimes on the floors of the rooms above the lily room. Also crying heard from inside the sealed room.
