Location: Insane asylum

Description:
On 24 mile and Card road there is a insane asylum in the woods that is haunted. It was built in 1929 and closed down in the early 80's. If you are to go there late at night you can hear screams and glass breaking in the rooms next to you if you enter. Also on the second floor there is light on in the window that is always on and you can see a shadow of a person in a straight jacket in the second floor window.- November Update: Has Been torn down
