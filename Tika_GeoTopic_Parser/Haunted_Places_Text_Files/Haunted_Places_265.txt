Location: Camp Lady By The Lake

Description:
A woman about 20 years ago ran a camp for orphans, and she went for a walk down the beach one day and when she came back the camps were burning and all the kids died, and she committed suicide, When You go there around 1 - 2 am, you can here screams and see the kids playing and you can see the women's ghost on the lake. A strange black apparition seems to follow or chase people.
