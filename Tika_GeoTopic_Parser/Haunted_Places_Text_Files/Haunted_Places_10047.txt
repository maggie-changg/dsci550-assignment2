Location: The Union Covered Bridge

Description:
It is said there was a death there in the mid- 1800's of a boy falling and drowning in the River. At night one can see the boy, and he will try to touch anyone who attempts to walk across the bridge in the dark. To get there Get on Hwy 15 and follow to Paris until the big brown signs say "Union Covered bridge." The brown signs will take you all the way to the correct spot
