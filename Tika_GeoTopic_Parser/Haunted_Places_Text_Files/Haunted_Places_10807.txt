Location: Rhode Island School of Design

Description:
Dunnel House -  -holds many spirits, the most menacing is in the basement. This ghost has been seen by many people and felt by almost everyone who has gone downstairs. It has been known to knock over trash cans, play with the light fixtures and television, and bring chills to anyone in the room. It occasionally appears in the form of a large shadow. In the back stairwell on the second floor, there resides a female spirit who appears to students through the window.
