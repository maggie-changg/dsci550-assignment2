Location: Old River Road

Description:
Old river road is a dangerous curvy road that is along the banks of the Hommochitto River. The road is a short cut to Franklin County High School, that many students take to go to school. The legend has it that in the 1950's a couple was returning home from a football game on a motorcycle and they did not see a sharp curve in the road and wrecked. Both died and the girl got decapitated. Every night at exactly 12:00 the boyfriend rides downs the road searching for his girlfriends head that he will never find...
