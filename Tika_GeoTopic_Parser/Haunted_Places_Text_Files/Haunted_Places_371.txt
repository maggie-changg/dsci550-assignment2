Location: Ethnic Creations

Description:
There are believed to be 3 ghosts living within the 3 floors of this building. 1 is said to be of a musician who lived in the building but committed suicide in the 1990's, the 2nd ghost stays in the basement and is believed to be of a woman, and the 3rd stays on the second floor by the air conditioner and is said to smell like rotten vegetables.
